Key analyst findings
⚠
Month-to-month contracts = 3× churn risk
43% churn rate vs. 0% on 2-year contracts. Immediate CS intervention needed for all M-t-M accounts with health score <50.
◷
Critical churn window: months 1–6
78% of churned accounts had tenure <6 months at time of churn. Onboarding quality is the single biggest retention lever.
✦
Feature adoption = strongest retention signal
Accounts using 5+ services have 2.4× higher health scores. Expansion plays and adoption programs directly reduce churn risk.
